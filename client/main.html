Bomb Your Friends

COMP4021 Project by Arnold, Patrick, and Joaquin



Based on the popular retro game "Bomberman", experience this classic party game right in your web browser!

INSTRUCTIONS


Objective

Place bombs to blow up blocks and your enemies! Grab powerups to become stronger.

Controls

Press WASD keys to move, press Spacebar to place a bomb.

Score and Ranking

Every match lasts 2 minutes. Defeat your enemy quickly to earn more points and rank high!
